Derrick

A Good Study Bible

I would enjoy having a study bible with a more scholarly translation to use in concert with the King James I already have. I often use The New Revised Standard Version (NRSV) and think it would best fit my needs. Both the Harper Collins and Oxford editions are well-regarded and their inclusion of apocryphal books is a definite plus. If I were to select an edition of either Bible, I would choose the older edition of the Oxford (which should be largely similar to the newest edition) or the student edition of the Harper Collins (which merely lacks the concordance of the more expensive version). Additionally, BYU Professors generally require either the Harper Collins or Oxford editions for their literary Bible courses and so both can be found on occasion in the textbook section of the BYU Store.

Sunglasses

Often when I am driving in the sun I end up borrowing Daphna's sunglasses so that I can see the road without having to squint. A simple pair of sunglasses would help us both be able to protect our eyes and drive without squinting. Since I already wear glasses, a pair that goes over ordinary glasses would be best.

Pajama Pants

My most recent pair of pajama pants have finally worn out. I now end up wearing gym shorts or track pants to bed on most nights. It would be nice to have a something a little warmer and softer (like fleece or flannel) pants to wear to bed. I ordinarly wear pants with a 34 inch waist and 32 inch length and prefer my pajamas comfortably loose.

Granta Magazine

When in doubt I always enjoy reading issues of Granta magazine. Old issues are usually quite cheap on Amazon and any issue from their entire print run is available on their website. I already own issues: 8, 31, 75, 112, 113, and 119.
